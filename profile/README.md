## 🐉 Welcome to the DragonMineZ Organization

We are the team behind **DragonMine Z**, an immersive Minecraft mod inspired by Akira Toriyama’s Dragon Ball universe, currently developed for Forge 1.20.1. 
Our focus is on building high-quality gameplay systems, thoughtful design, and tools that make the mod fun both in singleplayer and on dedicated servers.

In the future, we plan to evolve this organization into a proper **brand**, expanding beyond a single mod into a broader ecosystem of projects, tools, and community resources.
As we grow, this space will centralize our documentation, contribution guides, and any new experiments we launch.

### 👥 Core Team

- **Founders & Lead Developers** – Yuseix, ezShokkoh
- **Developer & Community Admin** – Bruno

We also collaborate with contributors, artists, builders, translators, and community members who help shape the DragonMine Z experience. The full list of contributors can be found in the mod's repository.

### 🚀 What We’re Working On

- Maintaining and expanding the DragonMine Z mod (systems, balance, content updates).
- Improving documentation and contribution flows so new developers can onboard easily. 
- Laying the groundwork for future projects under a unified brand identity.

### 🤝 Get Involved

If you are interested in contributing code, art, translations, or testing, check out our repositories and contribution docs, then open an issue or pull request.
You can also join our community via the links in the DragonMine Z mod page (Discord, docs, and more) to follow updates and talk with the team.
